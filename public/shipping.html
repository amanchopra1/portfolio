Shipping Information

Last updated: February 1, 2024

Order Processing

We aim to process all orders within 2-3 business days. Once your order has been processed, you will receive a confirmation email with the details of your shipment.

Shipping Timeframe

Our standard shipping timeframe is 5-10 business days from the date of order processing. Please note that delivery times may vary based on your location and other factors beyond our control.

Tracking Information

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to monitor the status and location of your package on our chosen shipping carrier's website.

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on the weight of your order and your delivery location. The exact shipping cost will be displayed during the checkout process before you complete your purchase.

International Shipping

We offer international shipping to select countries. International shipping times may vary, and additional customs fees or taxes may apply. Customers are responsible for any customs duties or taxes incurred upon delivery.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our shipping information, please contact our customer support at amanchopramotivation@gmail.com.
